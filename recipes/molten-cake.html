My Molten Cake Recipe

Description

Chocolate lava cakes with their deliciously rich molten chocolate center come together with 6 simple ingredients. Ready in just 25 minutes, this surprisingly easy dessert always impresses.

Ingredients

  • Eggs: 150 g (3 large)
  • Melted Butter: 60 g
  • Dream Molten Cake Powder: Box

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 170-180Â°C.
  2. Grease bottom of the four molten cake cups with butter.
  3. Empty the powder mix and add the eggs and the melted butter in a large bowl.
  4. Mix all using the electric mixer for 2 minutes at low speed, then for 3 minutes at high speed until well blended.
  5. Pour the mixture into the greased molten cake cups and bake in the oven for 13 to 17 minutes.
  6. Serve warm.
